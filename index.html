About us.
Tashkent University of Information Technologies (former Tashkent Electrotechnical Institute of Communications) was founded in 1955. On May 30, 2002, in accordance with the Decree № 3080 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan it was renamed into the Tashkent University of Information Technology. On June 2, 2005, according to the resolution № 91 by the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan regional branches of TUIT were formed in Karshi, Samarkand, Urgench and Fergana cities.
